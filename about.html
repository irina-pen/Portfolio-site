About

Inspired by human creation – visual arts, architecture, and fashion, the heartbeat behind my mission is to create meaningful user experiences through humanizing and aestheticizing digital products.

My Story

Artist turned UI designer. I used to draw, paint, research and analyze. My academic background in Fine arts gives me an intrinsic understanding of composition and the feeling of color. Experience in Research, Analyses, Project curation, writing, and Public speaking has taught me to develop a project from idea to accomplished product. 

Embracing the world. After moving from my home country Kazakhstan to Japan and now to France, traveling in Europe and the US in between, I felt it was a time for me to put aside the theory and history of arts as my own work history and move to intangible, to digital, to creation.

Searching for Golden ratio. Experience of living in different countries let me embrace values common to humanity unifying and nurturing Kazakh tolerance, Japanese perfectionism, and French style. Thanks to the years of scrutinizing the worlds’ best creations, I developed both an intrinsic appreciation for aesthetics and the empathy needed to grasp the real meaning that the artist wanted to convey in his artwork. I want to make a difference by producing visual designs that are both beautiful and user-friendly.

Currently I am available for full-time roles in the South of France.
