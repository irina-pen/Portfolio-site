Wtravel LOCATION BASED RECOMMENDATION APP

Duration of the project: april-august 2020

Roles: UI & UX design

View prototype

About

Wtravel is a location-based recommendation application for parents who want to stay active even with a small kid. Wtravel finds desired destinations and builds the most convenient itineraries regarding the preference of a traveler.

What was designed:

  1. Mobile
  2. Tablet
  3. Desktop

Objective

The app aiming to help travelers with kids to organize their trips and support them during the road. That includes fast access to the information on kids’ friendly facilities, restrooms, emergency rooms. The app allows to choose the type of itinerary - stroller-friendly or not, it accommodates the user’s preference for hiking difficulty and desired places to visit.

Work with brief

"Users of the web are hugely dependent on the use of maps. Maps for navigation from A to B and, increasingly, maps for location-based recommendations… Your solution involves the creation of a responsive web app that will meet these needs and solve the problems that users are currently facing".

Gathering requirements

Questions

1. Who is the user ?
2. What major goals, tasks will users want/ need to accomplish?
3. What will the product do?
4. Where will the product be used?
5. Why does the user need the product?
6. What kind of information will be created, entered or featured in the product?
7. What particular business goals are you trying to achieve?

Answers

  1. Parents, caregivers that travel with their kids.
  2. Plan the kid friendly trip, get the suggestions and information during the way, easy search of places far and nearby.
  3. The app will suggest and save the itineraries according the preferences of spots to visit, would be flexible to iteration of the plan during the trip as travel with kids can be unpredictable.
  4. At home and during the trip.
  5. To facilitate his life as on preparation the trip and when on the road.
  6. Planning and organizing the kid friendly travels. Easy and quick search of kid friendly facilities and places of interests, user navigation from point A to point B.
  7. The brand recognition through travelers, the number of active users, the attracting of advertising.

Final Designs

Four reasons to love Wtravel

1. Easy sign in with your password or social media account.

2. Fast access to the favorite places. Visit it now or save for later.

3. Do you want to improvise while on an itinerary? Just look what's around on the map.

4. Now it is possible to create an itinerary together. Just share it with a friend and co-create.

We prioritize efficiency 

AR Navigation for the best support. No more unclear maps and obscure directions. Our mascot bird will bring you the destination most shortly and conveniently.
